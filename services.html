    • Our Services

    Direct Capital offers tailored financing solutions, including Local Purchase Order (LPO) Financing, Property Bridge Financing, Purchase Order (PO) Financing, Contract Financing, and Invoice Discounting. We ensure you have the funds you need to keep your business running smoothly. Empower your growth with Direct Capital today.

    Government Purchase Orders

    Direct Capital's Purchase Order (PO) financing provides cash for businesses based on a client's purchase order, unlike traditional lenders that focus solely on the borrower's financial status. This type of financing supports the procurement or production of goods pre-sold to reliable buyers.

    By offering flexible terms, we help businesses take on larger orders and compete more effectively. PO financing is often paired with factoring programs to ensure continuous cash flow throughout the trading process, enabling companies to meet contractual obligations and pursue additional opportunities.

    Local Purchase Orders

    Local Purchase Order (LPO) financing from Direct Capital offers businesses several advantages. Customers can quickly obtain the cash needed to fulfill orders without waiting for client payments, optimizing their financial position and preventing delivery delays that could harm their reputation. Direct Capital also provides customized repayment periods tailored to each business's needs, reducing financial strain and simplifying cash flow management.

    Additionally, our LPO loans feature flexible interest rates, helping businesses save on interest costs and improve profitability for future growth. Our LPO Support finances local purchase orders or agreements for the supply or delivery of specified goods or services as outlined in the contract.

    Contract Financing

    Direct Capital offers contract financing for businesses awarded contracts but lacking the immediate cash to execute them. By using the contract's value as collateral, we provide advances against upcoming invoices, allowing businesses to pay employees, suppliers, and cover additional expenses.

    The financing primarily depends on the end customer's creditworthiness rather than the business's. This enables businesses to undertake large projects with confidence, knowing they have the necessary financial support.

    Invoice Discounting

    Invoice discounting from Direct Capital allows businesses to unlock cash tied up in unpaid invoices. This provides quick access to working capital, improving cash flow for business expansion or supplier payments.

    Unlike traditional financial institutions, our approval process is faster and based on individual invoices rather than credit history. Clients benefit from flexible, short-term financing without long-term, high-interest commitments.

    Property Bridging Finance

    Property bridge loans from Direct Capital, tailored for Botswana, provide short-term financing for various property-related transactions, including building, renovating, or purchasing properties. These loans are secured by the sold property and based on the net sale proceeds.

    Bridge financing is beneficial for property owners needing immediate funds for renovations, down payments, or to cover outstanding levies and taxes. This ensures seamless property transactions and helps sellers secure new properties promptly.
